Hi, I'm Kelly! I've been playing the piano since I was seven years old.

About Me

I'm a Computer Science major. I'm not part of George Fox's music program, but I am taking piano lessons for fun.

I began taking lessons when I was seven years old. My mom was the one who first suggested it to me, becuase she was tired of hearing me play "Mary Had a Little Lamb" nonstop and wanted me to learn something else.

Piano has always been a hobby of mine more than anything else. I don't plan to ever play professionally. But I appreciate the variety of experiences playing the piano brings me that I would never encounter otherwise.

Favorite Memories

Some of my favorite experiences playing the piano are:

  • Playing in small recitals growing up
  • Playing for residents at a local retirement home
  • Teaching young beginning piano students
  • Playing regularly for a local church in my hometown
  • Playing a piano duo together with my fiancé
